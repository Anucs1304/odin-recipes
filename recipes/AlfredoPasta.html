Alfredo Pasta

Description

Alfredo Pasra is one of the easy to prepare Italian dish. Any one with basic cooking skills can make it at home. It is a creamy and cheesy in texture and also called white sauce pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Refined oil
  • Butter
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Chopped Garlic
  • Fresh Cream
  • Grated Cheese
  • Crushed Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil water and add salt, pasta.
  2. In a pan, add oil, butter, chilli flakes.
  3. Add chopped garlic to that.
  4. After frying those, add fresh cream and stir it.
  5. After the pasta cooks well, add the pasta to the thick sauce we made.
  6. Add grated cheese and salt if in need.
  7. Add crushed pepper and Italian seasoning.
  8. Loose the pasta with water if in need and serve.
