Chicken Biryani

Description

Chiclen biryani an aromatic, spicy South Asian one-pot dish featuring tender, yogurt-marinated chicken slow-cooked with fragrant basmati rice, caramelized onions, and exotic spices. Traditionally cooked using the dum (steaming) method, it is a flavorful, rich rice meal often garnished with mint and cilantro.

Ingredients

For marinating the chicken

  • Chicken
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Chilli Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice / yogurt

For deep frying onion

  • Onion
  • Refined Oil

For frying Cashew Nuts and Raisins

  • Ghee
  • Cashew Nut
  • Raisins

For cooking chicken

  • Refined Oil
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Cloves
  • Cardamom
  • Star Anise
  • Mace
  • Bay Leaf
  • Green Chilli
  • Onion
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • salt
  • Coriander Powder
  • Chilli powder
  • Garam masala
  • Crushed black pepper
  • Hot Water

For cooking the rice

  • Water
  • Bay Leaf
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cloves
  • Cardamom
  • Lime juice
  • Refined Oil
  • Salt
  • Basmati rice

For the dum process

  • Mint Leaves
  • Ghee

steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with the given ingredients for 1 to 3 hours.
  2. Wash the basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes.
  3. Fry 2 sliced onions.
  4. Fry cashew and Raisins.
  5. Heat the oil and roast Cinnamon, Cardamom, cloves, mace, bay leaf and star anise.
  6. Add chopped green chilli, onion, ginger garlic paste and saute them.
  7. Add the powders.
  8. Add the marinated chicken into that.
  9. Add 3/4 cup of hot water and stir well.
  10. Close the lid and cook for 10-15 minutes.
  11. To prepare rice, to 2 and 1/4 water, put cardamom, cloves, Cinnamon, bay leaf, lime juice, oil, salt and fried onion.
  12. Add the soaked rice to the water and close the lid.
  13. Add mint leaves to the chicken and layer the rice over the mint leaves.
  14. add fried cashew, raisins, fried onions and ghee.
  15. Close the lid and dum in lowest heat for 15 minutes.
  16. Cool the biryani down and serve it.
