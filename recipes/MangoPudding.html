Mango Pudding

Description

Mango Pudding is a delightful dessert that captivates with its smooth texture and vibrant flavour. It is not only visually appealing but also a refreshing finale to any meal, transporting taste buds to sun-kissed orchards and warm summer days with each spoonful.

Ingredients

  • Mango
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Corn Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the rip mango.
  2. Grind mango, milk and sugar smoothly.
  3. Mix corn flour and milk well.
  4. Heat pan in low flame, add the grind mango to the pan and stri continuously.
  5. Add the corn flour and milk to the pan little by little and stri continuously.
  6. Add pinch of salt.
  7. Take glass or cups and transfer.
  8. close it with foil paper and put it in the refrigerator for atleast two to three hours.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
